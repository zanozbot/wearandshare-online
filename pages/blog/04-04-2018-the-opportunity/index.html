The sharing culture is spreading. Ideas like convenience, lower cost of living, connecting with others, etc. are the reasons more and more young people are taking a liking towards sharing.

At the same time nearly half the women state they don’t have the desired outfits for the activities they want to attend, while owning on average more than a hundred wearables and wearing only about 20% regularly.

A glaring opportunity appears when these two facts are combined. Let’s make sharing clothing a breeze. Exactly how we want to disrupt the fashion industry by introducing Wear & Share.

We have seen numerous people visiting each other and simply borrowing wearables from each other. But this only happened when they visited each others homes. What if we could bring their home to you? What if we could incorporate their wardrobe into yours?

With Wear & Share we can.

With this ever increasing virtual wardrobe, another opportunity appears. Such a huge number of wearables might prove to be quite a challenge to go through.

Should our users feel overwhelmed, an AI-enabled service will be offered to them, where outfits will be genereated for them, based on their likes, dislikes, preffered types, colors, fits and other characteristics.

Are you going to a party, a fancy dinner, a gala event or even just a simple date? Whatever it is, Wear & Share will provide you with a perfect outfit.

Both these opportunities and more will be capitalized on with the mobile Wear & Share application.

Wear & Share is the first distributed peer-to-peer mobile application for sharing your wardrobe among friends. To learn everything about the application and more, take a peek at our website or connect with us on social media.
