Share

Take pictures of your wearables to store them in your virtual wardrobe. Add friends and family to combine and expand your wardrobe. Browse and borrow from a huge joint wardrobe using just one finger.

Organize

Add information and tags such as color, size, season and brand to your wearables to organize your wardrobe to your liking. Have everything where you want it. And find it with ease when you need it.

Follow

Follow friends on your in-app feed. See how your clothing completes their look. See and borrow new additions to their wardrobe. Let your friends know about the wearables you love by commenting and liking.

Discover

Let us combine outfits for you. Our machine learning algorithms will learn your likes and dislikes to create your perfect look. All you have to do to take everybody’s breath away is complete the outfit by borrowing missing wearables from friends.

Group up

Create a private group for sharing wearables share to specific friends only. Or use groups to create themed wardrobes for you and your friends. Attending an event with a specific dress code? Sounds like a new group is forming.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I download the app?

The application is currently not released to the public yet. The best option for you right now is to register to our newsletter and follow us on social media to get notified as soon as it’s available to you.

2. How do wearables get transfered?

You and your friend are responsible for the exchange of wearables. Set a time and place you both agree on and exchange it there and then. Make sure you both share wearables with each other to be more efficient.

3. What happens if wearables get damaged?

Our platform relies on trust between friends as wearables are only shared among people you know. Should something be damaged it’s up to those two friends to come to an agreement concerning the damaged goods.

4. How are outfits generated?

We learn from your likes and dislikes on our platform. We also consider your preferred types, colors, fits and other characteristics to provide you with an outfit we believe you will like the best.
